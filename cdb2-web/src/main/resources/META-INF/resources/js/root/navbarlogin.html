Welcome to Find a Bailiff Directory !

The Find a Bailiff applet will enable you to create, in a few clicks, your National Directory of Bailiffs/Enforcement authorities to be connected to the European Find a Bailiff Directory. Thus, European citizens and companies will be able to identify, while browsing on Find a Bailiff Directory, the competent Bailiff/Enforcement authority to solve their case in your Member State.
